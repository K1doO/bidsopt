Engineering Team

Bidsopt Engineering works on the most complex and interesting problems of real time bidding. We're constantly expanding and updating our real-time bidding platform, giving advertisers the tools to take charge of their own ad campaigns.We're looking for Software Engineer to take care of our massive real time bidding system. We receive millions of ad requests on our bidders daily, each one requiring a lookup of our billions of users and a response in milliseconds. We have built our stack on Amazon Web Services (AWS) with some of the most exciting new performance technologies, including Java, Pig, Hadoop, RDS,No Sql ,Python etc

Ad Operations Team

Our Ad Operation team executes and optimizes campaigns for our managed service customers. We offer managed support across custom creative implementations, data and analytics, and end-to-end campaign management.

Business Development Team

The Business Development team onboards and integrates advertisers , exchanges and publishers onto the Bidsopt platform. This team is focused at understanding the challenges and then delivering the best mobile advertising solutions to meet requirements.
